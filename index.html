Power, peace and belonging

We offer courses for people who care about making parts of the world work.  We specialize in empowering people who have a large vision for the world, but who occasionally see themselves as small.  The focus of our courses is the entrenched nature of institutions.
People find out about our courses from friends in their network who recommend the courses to them. We rely entirely on the reputation we’ve established that way.
Our courses are delivered through Conference Call telephone lines.  The sessions are 90 minutes long.  The courses are free of charge.  Our entry course Grace with Institutions has no prerequisites.
